TVI Commercial & Licensing Notes

Strategic Framework for Commercial Integration

When a License Is Required

A commercial license is required for any use of TVI where outputs inform business decisions, client work, or operational systems. Specifically:

  • Investment decisions: When TVI scores inform capital allocation, portfolio management, or investment analysis
  • Brand / media / marketing analytics: When TVI is used for brand impact measurement, media valuation, marketing effectiveness analysis, or client-facing analytics
  • Client deliverables: When TVI analysis is included in reports, presentations, or deliverables provided to clients or external stakeholders
  • Internal decision systems: When TVI is integrated into internal analytics, dashboards, or decision-support systems that inform operational choices
  • Dashboards, pipelines, automation: When TVI is embedded in automated workflows, data pipelines, or dashboard systems
  • API or batch usage: When TVI is accessed programmatically, used for bulk analysis, or integrated into other systems via API

Demonstrative use (public calculator, evaluation access) does not require a license. Any operational, client-facing, or decision-informing use requires commercial licensing.

Commercial Use Cases

TVI is designed for integration into existing analytical workflows and platforms. Primary commercial use cases include:

  • Investment & research firms: Downside protection, structural validation, and temporal advantage analysis for portfolio management
  • Brand and marketing analytics providers: Long-term brand impact measurement, cultural measurement, and media effectiveness analysis beyond short-term engagement metrics
  • Media intelligence companies: Content valuation, cultural persistence analysis, and long-horizon trend identification
  • Cultural analytics platforms: Temporal validation layers for content recommendation, curation, and archival prioritization
  • AI / data organizations: Dataset durability assessment, benchmark staying power evaluation, and training data selection
  • Strategy & advisory firms: Methodology validation, framework durability analysis, and long-term strategic planning support

TVI functions as a validation layer and measurement standard, not a standalone product. It is designed for integration into existing analytical infrastructure or strategic acquisition by established metrics providers.

Licensed Access (Conceptual)

Commercial licensing provides decision-grade access to TVI. Licensed access includes (conceptually):

  • Decision-grade outputs: Validated, calibrated TVI scores appropriate for operational use, investment analysis, and client deliverables
  • Batch analysis: Capabilities for analyzing multiple entities, portfolios, or datasets at scale
  • API access: Programmatic access to TVI calculation and analysis (conceptual—specific implementation depends on integration requirements)
  • Internal or client-facing rights: Usage rights for internal decision systems or client-facing analytics and deliverables
  • Optional white-label deployment: Ability to deploy TVI under your brand for client-facing applications (subject to agreement)
  • Advisory support: Optional consultation on TVI interpretation, integration, and best practices (subject to agreement)

Specific licensing terms, access levels, and integration capabilities are determined through commercial discussion based on use case, scale, and integration requirements.

IP & Ownership

TVI is proprietary. The Temporal Validation Index framework, including its calculation methodology, era coefficients, domain-specific adjustments, classification thresholds, and all related analytical logic, is proprietary to BoonMind Analytics.

Patent applications in preparation. TVI is protected by patent applications in preparation. Licensing does not transfer IP ownership unless explicitly agreed in writing.

Licensing ≠ ownership. A commercial license grants usage rights for defined purposes and scopes. It does not transfer ownership of the TVI framework, methodology, or IP.

Any use of TVI beyond demonstrative evaluation requires explicit licensing. Reverse engineering, replication, or unauthorized use of TVI methodology is prohibited.

Strategic Positioning

TVI is positioned as:

  • A measurement standard, not a dashboard: TVI provides a validated framework for temporal validation, designed to become a reference standard rather than a user-facing product
  • A validation layer, not a growth metric: TVI measures durability and persistence, complementing (not replacing) engagement and growth metrics
  • Designed for integration or acquisition: TVI is structured for integration into existing analytical platforms or strategic acquisition by established metrics and analytics providers

TVI is not positioned as a standalone SaaS product, consumer tool, or growth-focused startup. It is an analytical asset designed for strategic integration.

Engagement Flow

Commercial engagement follows a structured flow:

  1. Evaluation: Assessment of TVI's relevance and fit through evaluation access and materials
  2. Licensing: Commercial discussion and agreement on licensing terms, scope, and access levels
  3. Deployment or Strategic Integration: Implementation of licensed access, API integration, or strategic integration into existing platforms

For strategic acquisition discussions, the flow may differ and is handled through direct engagement with BoonMind Analytics leadership.
