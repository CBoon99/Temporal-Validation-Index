Research Opportunities

Prospective Prediction Study

The strongest test: rank novel entities by TVI today, measure persistence at 1, 5, and 10 year intervals. We propose a registered study tracking 100 entities across domains.

Observer Calibration Experiments

Test whether observers with different τ profiles make systematically different forecasts. Hypothesis: high-V observers outperform on long-horizon outcomes; low-V observers outperform on short-horizon outcomes.

Cross-Domain Expansion

Adapt TVI framework to additional domains: scientific papers, patents, policy frameworks, music, art, social movements.

Empirical Parameter Calibration

Improve Account Factor estimates through user surveys; validate Structural Resistance Coefficients through historical analysis.
